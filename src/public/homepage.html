Barzinho - 30 years of everything

It’s the history, the space, the music, the drinks and the energy. Barzinho has been the standard for the nightlife in Ribamar and the forefront of life music in Ericeira for years. Opened 30 years ago, Barzinho has known how to grow organically with it’s surroundings and it’s people. Starting off as a tropical bar influenced by Afonso’s travels in Brazil, it has surely transformed itself as a home. A place where people can enjoy the later part of the day anyway they please be it singing and dancing with the crowd, playing a serious game of pool, listening to the soft melodies of the musicians or enjoying a quiet glass of wine in the outside area. Barzinho is prepared to welcome any kind of people. Nicely prepared cocktails or a mean pint of beer. A jazzy tune or a Rock Riff. Friendly staff will definitely show you the way. See you soon.
